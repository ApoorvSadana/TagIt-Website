How we do it?

#

Customized Challenge

We create a challenge and a hashtag specific to your company. Everytime a user comes to our platofrm, he sees what your company is doing for charity.

Performing the Challenge

Users do your challenge in the form of a video or photo. All media is watermarked with your hashtag and the charity that is being done behind it.

Exponential Growth

Everytime the user does the challenge, he tags his friends to come and participate. The challengers and outreach grow exponentially over time!

We make the difference!

Once we touch the upper cap, we stop the challenge and make the donation. We also reward our top influencers with goodies and coupons from your side.
